We always try our best to challenge your artistic abilities and produce some interesting and unique artwork, and as designers, we usually turn to different sources of inspiration. As a matter of fact, we’ve discovered the best one: <strong>desktop wallpapers</strong> that are a little more distinctive than the usual crowd.

This creativity mission <a href="https://www.smashingmagazine.com/tag/wallpapers/">has been going on for eight years now</a>, and we are very thankful to all designers who have contributed to it and who are still enthusiastically continuing to do so each month. This post features free desktop wallpapers created by artists across the globe for June 2016. Both versions with and without a calendar can be downloaded for free. It’s time to give your desktop a makeover!

## Designer’s Road Trip

“For me, June means that I have a break from college, and the weekends usually involve road trips with friends. As a graphic design major, I always thought it would be funny to have a license plate that says ‘kerning’, because the letters on the plate split in the middle and leave a gap between them (what <em>kerning</em> is in design terminology). I’m a sucker for graphic design puns, as you can see. So I created a car with that license plate, driving through a winding road on a summer day. In the distance are also cursor-shaped trees which allude to web design. Because I am a student of the subject, I can never really stop thinking about design, even during summer vacation!” — Designed by <a href="https://www.mu-art.org/students/whitaker">Katlynn Whitaker</a> from the the United States.
