But still, dozens of years later, many artists use black and white photography for artistic purposes. It is one of the best and cheapest types of photography for the beginners to test their creativity. This showcase is a tribute to this often forgotten and underrated art of photography.

In this post we present <strong>beautiful examples of black and white photography</strong> — an update to previously featured <a title="Beautiful Black and White Photography" href="https://www.smashingmagazine.com/2008/06/09/beautiful-black-and-white-photography/" rel="bookmark">showcase of black and white photography</a>. The showcase below focuses more on art and creative mind of photographers. Of course, it's not the ultimate collection. There were many more which we could not cover in a single post. All the photographs are linked to photographer’s website. Please do visit their sites to explore the skills and craft of these artists.</p>
