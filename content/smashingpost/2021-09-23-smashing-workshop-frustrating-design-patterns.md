**Disabled buttons.** Infinite scroll. Poor inline validation. Parallax. Carousels. Modals. Mega-dropdown hover menus. There is plenty of frustration on the web. Let‚Äôs fix that. Join us for a [free online workshop on Frustrating Design Patterns](https://smashingconf.com/online-workshops/workshops/vitaly-friedman-frustrating-patterns/) on **Monday, September 27 at 9:00 AM PDT / 6PM CET**. 

In the **2.5h live session** we‚Äôll take a close look at some of the confusing and annoying patterns and explore better alternatives alongside plenty of examples and checklists to keep in mind when building or designing one. You‚Äôll **walk away with a toolbox of techniques** and examples of doing things well &mdash; in your product, website, desktop app or mobile app.

We‚Äôll look into carousels, modals, infinite scroll, parallax and scrolljacking, mega-dropdown menus, disabled buttons, inline validation, frozen filters, CAPTCHA, authentication and privacy.
