<p>“In Belgium remember the Three Wise Men of the East is a tradition. It involves children going from door to door, dressed up as the three Wise Men. They sing a little song, in exchange for sweets and/or money. The design is very minimalistic and &lsquo;flat&rsquo;. I hope you like it :)” &mdash; Designed by <a href="https://www.iamjer.be">Jeroen Bartels</a> from Belgium.</p>
