A good freebie is always good to have. Today, we're happy to release a quite unique icon set. The goodie contains 92 icons in three editable variants: outlined, filled &amp; colored, covering 15 wide categories: Science, Medical, Tech, Kitchen, Activities, Care, Weather, Marketing, Transport, Holidays, Formats, Interface, Interaction, Photo, Brands. 

Using a precise grid of 60px to guarantee sharpness and consistency, unlimited possibilities are unlocked when your creativity applies to this awesome set. Multiple formats available : AI, Sketch, PNG, SVG, EPS, PDF.
