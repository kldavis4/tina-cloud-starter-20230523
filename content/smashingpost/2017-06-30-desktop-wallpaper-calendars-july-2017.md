## The Sunny South

“The wallpaper is based on a picture I took when I was on vacation last year with my parents. I took it in Bormes-les-Mimosas in the South of France. It’s a very lovely village. Being there always makes me happy, so I wanted to share this feeling with the world.” — Designed by [Melissa Bogemans](https://melissa.bogemans.com) from Belgium.
